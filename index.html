Important Notes

Please read these important instructions before placing your order

📋

Order Guidelines

  • • Please ensure your PDF file is not password protected and is less than 100MB in size.
  • • All orders are processed within 2-4 hours during business hours (7 AM - 7 PM).
  • • For urgent orders, please contact us directly on Telegram for faster processing.
  • • We do NOT accept last-minute orders. Please place your order at least 1–2 days in advance to ensure timely delivery.
💰

Payment Information

  • • Payment should be made during the form filling process itself.
  • • Payment should be made during the form filling process via UPI using the provided QR code.
  • • Always upload the payment screenshot in the Google Form itself for verification.
📦

Delivery Details

  • • Free delivery within college campus during working hours.
🔄

Cancellation Policy And Service Downtime During Exams

  • • Orders can be cancelled within 30 minutes of placement without any charges by contacting us on Telegram.
  • • If printing has already started, 50% of the order value will be charged.
  • • Refunds will be processed within 24-48 hours of cancellation approval.
  • • The website/service will be temporarily unavailable during unit tests (only for the test duration).
  • • 📅 For final semester exams, the website will be taken offline exactly 24 days before the exam date.
  • • No orders will be accepted during this period, so please plan and place your orders well in advance.
